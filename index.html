Popular projects in EGYPT.

New-Zayed-city.
The new Sheikh Zayed City is an extension of Sheikh Zayed and is located on an area of ​​21 thousand acres northwest of Sheikh Zayed City and was established to be the size of Sheikh Zayed City to accommodate a larger number of residents of the two cities, reaching 650,000 people, double the current population, as the new Sheikh Zayed City It includes many social and luxury residential projects, economic housing and youth housing, and lands will be offered to citizens and investors. The new Sheikh Zayed is distinguished by its distinctive planning in terms of green spaces and wide roads, which include many facilities and service areas according to the citizen needs
New-Mansura-city.
New Mansoura City is one of the cities affiliated to the New Urban Communities Authority, which was built according to a republican decree in 2017 to be used to build a new urban community. It was scheduled to be finalized by 2018.
New-Alamin-city.
The Minister of Housing demonstrated the project as shown in the attached video (from 38:30 to 48:29). - The New El-Alamein City is an integrated city at the heart of the North West Coast. - New El-Alamein City includes all residential activities, cultural, tourist, industrial and research areas, a private university, international hotels, two presidential and cabinet buildings. - It is implemented on a total area of 48, 917 thousand feddans. - The targeted number of residents are nearly 3 million people.
